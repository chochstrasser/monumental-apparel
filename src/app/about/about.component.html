About Us

Our company was founded in a college dorm in Provo, Utah. Nathan had been doing graphic design and illustration for a couple of years, and decided it was time to start selling his art. Gabe joined with Nathan to help get the company going. Nathan is in charge of marketing and design and Gabe is in charge of finance and logistics. We wanted to do more than just sell clothes, though. We wanted to create a movement dedicated to everything we thought was worth loving about the world. The only word we could think of was 'Monumental'. This word grew into a passionate, environmentally conscious brand, dedicated to all that is monumental.
